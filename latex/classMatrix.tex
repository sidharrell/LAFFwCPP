\subsection{Detailed Description}
The class \hyperlink{classMatrix}{Matrix} should be able to generate zero and identity matrices and instantiate \hyperlink{classMatrix}{Matrix} objects that can operate on themselves, and perform G\-E\-M\-V and G\-E\-M\-M operations.
